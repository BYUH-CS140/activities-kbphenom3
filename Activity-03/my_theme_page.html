What You Should Know When You Visit Japan (for about 2 weeks)

DOs

Passport:

Really goes without saying that a passport is necessary to legally fly over there...

IC Card: An IC card application can also be downloaded on iPhones for free

eSIM: Buy a temporary eSIM for internet connection on the go.

Light amount of clothes: pack light overall

Don'ts

Don't bring a lot of basic toiletries: Even the cheapest of Japanese hotels provide towels, blankets and pillows, toothbrushes and toothpaste, soap and shampoo.

Too many clothes: leave enough room in suitcase for possible souvenirs.
