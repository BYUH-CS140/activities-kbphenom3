Blue Ridge Mountains

The Blue Ridge Mountains are a mountain range in the eastern United States, part of the Appalachian Mountains. Kinda like Utah's Wasatch Mountains while they are part of the Rocky Mountains. These Blue Ridge Mountains are known for the huge spread of vast scenic views full of hikes and curvy roads, as well as their diverse, colorful trees.
