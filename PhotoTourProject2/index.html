Kanaan's Virginia Photo Tour

Welcome to Virginia, where the slogan is "Virginia is for Lovers". Virginia is located next to the Atlantic Ocean, and it is next door to the United States' capital city, Washington D.C. The scenery is modest and mostly rural with the most urban spots being towards the north towards D.C. or the ocean side.
