Charlottesville

Charlottesville is a town of 47000 people located in ALbemarle County of Central Virginia. While Charlottesville isn't the biggest town in VA, it still has everything you need to have quality time for the short or long term. In the outskirts of Charlottesville is the famous Monticello, the house of former US President, Thomas Jefferson. Within Charlottesvillle is the University of Virginia or UVA for short, known for winning the Division I NCAA Basketball National Championship in 2019.
